Playing under the name Manic subsidiary, in 1985 the band decided to change their name to The Offspring. Their first single, "I'll Be Waiting/Blackball", was released on vinyl by independent label Black Label Records and sold 1,000 copies.

Their debut album, The Offspring, was released on the independent label Nemesis Records in 1989 and produced by Thom Wilson, selling three thousand copies of this vinyl debut
